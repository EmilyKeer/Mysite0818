Glimpse

Situated in the North of China, Inner Mongolia never ceases to attract visitors and backpackers from far and wide. When it comes to Inner Mongolia, it is likely for people to think of wide and expanded grasslands with countless cattle everywhere, strong and frank natives whom are easy to talk and stay with, as well as other fascinating and mystical places of interests and legends related. If you have been bored with the dull and busy city life, or if you are dreaming of exploring a marvelous destination along with exploring a better self, it is no denying that Inner Mongolia is an appropriate choice for you!

Beautiful Nature

As a famous song by the popular singer Jamyang Dolma sings, "my love is along the horizon / where there is a wide and large prairie / in the boundless and indistinct world / white yurts are lying alongside the river." Inner Mongolia has beautiful and attractive prairies which are fascinated by both local people and travelers there. One of the renowned prairies in Inner Mongolia is Hulun Buir Prairie. When you set foot on the prairie, you can feel you are right covered by a mast pure-blue sky with spotless white clouds dotted. How wonderful it is to enjoy the perfect interaction and combination of the sky and the grasslands, which makes you drowned in the illusion that you are in the heaven. The delicate fragrance of grass and flowers is charming enough to attract you to explore the pure and beautiful nature.
